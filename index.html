ROADMAP

Where Lumen is going

This demo is the seed. Here's the shape of the full product.

SHIPPED · v0.1

Interactive knowledge graph

Tag-based auto-linking, force-directed layout, zoom/pan, click-to-open, and a polished notes viewer. Add your own notes — they persist in your browser.

SHIPPED · v0.2

In-browser semantic search

Real sentence embeddings (MiniLM-L6-v2) run in your browser. Toggle to "Semantic" in the graph to see meaning-based connections — no data leaves your machine.

NEXT · v0.3

Ask your notes

Type a question. Lumen finds the most relevant chunks, shows you exactly what it will send, and returns a cited answer via an LLM of your choice.

LATER · v0.4

Ingest anything

Drop a PDF, paste a URL, or connect a folder. Lumen extracts, summarizes, and weaves the new knowledge into your graph.

ROADMAP

Where Lumen is going

This demo is the seed. Here's the shape of the full product.

SHIPPED · v0.1

Interactive knowledge graph

Tag-based auto-linking, force-directed layout, zoom/pan, click-to-open, and a polished notes viewer. Add your own notes — they persist in your browser.

SHIPPED · v0.2

In-browser semantic search

Real sentence embeddings (MiniLM-L6-v2) run in your browser. Toggle to "Semantic" in the graph to see meaning-based connections from local vectors.

SHIPPED · v0.3

Ask your notes

Retrieval-augmented Q&A. Embeddings find relevant passages, the context is shown to you before sending, and the answer cites source notes. Bring your own free OpenRouter key.

SHIPPED · v0.4

Ingest anything

Drop a PDF or paste a URL. PDFs are parsed locally; URLs are fetched through r.jina.ai. New content is chunked, embedded, and woven into your graph.

SHIPPED · v0.5

Quality Lab

Chunked retrieval, inspectable prompts, local feedback, and a public benchmark that compares chunk retrieval against the older whole-note baseline.

What leaves your machine
  • Ask: only the retrieved chunks shown in the inspector are sent to OpenRouter with your API key.
  • URL import: the URL you paste is sent to r.jina.ai to fetch a readable copy.
  • PDF import, notes, embeddings, eval runs, and feedback stay in this browser unless you export or send them.

ROADMAP

Where Lumen is going

This demo is the seed. Here's the shape of the full product.

SHIPPED · v0.1

Interactive knowledge graph

Tag-based auto-linking, force-directed layout, zoom/pan, click-to-open, and a polished notes viewer. Add your own notes — they persist in your browser.

SHIPPED · v0.2

In-browser semantic search

Real sentence embeddings (MiniLM-L6-v2) run in your browser. Toggle to "Semantic" in the graph to see meaning-based connections — no data leaves your machine.

SHIPPED · v0.3

Ask your notes

Retrieval-augmented Q&A. Embeddings find relevant passages, the context is shown to you before sending, and the answer cites source notes. Bring your own free OpenRouter key.

SHIPPED · v0.4

Ingest anything

Drop a PDF or paste a URL. Lumen extracts the text in your browser (PDFs never leave your machine), embeds the new content, and weaves it into your graph instantly.

NEXT · v0.5

Bring your own folder

Optional sync via a Dropbox or iCloud folder you choose. Your notes stay yours — Lumen still stores nothing on our side.
